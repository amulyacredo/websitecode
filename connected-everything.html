Critical Infrastructure

Power, pipelines, and production — uninterrupted and uncompromised.

In oil & gas and other heavy industries, even seconds of downtime can mean millions lost. Our homogenic assurance and continuous controls testing run in parallel with operations, ensuring OT and IT systems are protected against ransomware, insider threats, and supply-chain exploits. Linked intelligence connects threats to asset context instantly, so operators can see and stop an incident before it hits the SCADA core.
