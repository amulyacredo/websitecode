Who We Are

CredO is a cybersecurity intelligence company dedicated to protecting organizations from advanced threats through innovative AI-powered solutions and real-time threat detection.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by cybersecurity veterans who witnessed firsthand the limitations of traditional security approaches, CredO was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create intelligent cybersecurity solutions that adapt and evolve with emerging threats.

After experiencing multiple sophisticated attacks that bypassed conventional security tools, our founders realized that the cybersecurity industry needed a fundamental shift toward behavioral analytics and real-time threat intelligence.
